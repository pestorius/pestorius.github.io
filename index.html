About Me

Travelling from the other side of the globe, I have come a far way from Malaysia to achieve my goals and explore new opportunities in Chicago. I began my journey in Oakton Community College, where I dived into the basics of Computer Science. 2 years later I transferred to Illinois Institute of Technology to pursue my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. I am currently an Illinois Institute of Technology alumni, and I have gained a vast knowledge of techniques and practices in Computer Science and related fields. Shortly after graduation I started work as a Software Test Engineer/Automation Developer at BAE Systems Applied Intelligence in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
